# Adidas Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Adidas sales performance across multiple business dimensions. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights, enabling stakeholders to evaluate business performance, identify trends, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

Adidas generates sales through multiple regions, retailers, product categories, and sales channels. Without a centralized reporting system, it becomes difficult to monitor performance, identify profitable business areas, and make informed strategic decisions.

---

## Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Adidas sales data and provide actionable insights by answering key business questions such as:

* Which regions generate the highest sales?
* Which product categories contribute the most revenue?
* Which retailers deliver the highest operating profit?
* Which sales methods perform best?
* How do sales trends change over time?

---

## Dashboard Features

* Total Sales KPI
* Operating Profit KPI
* Operating Margin KPI
* Unit Price KPI
* Sales by Product Category
* Sales by Region
* Operating Profit by Retailer
* Sales by Sales Method
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Interactive Filters (City & Gender)

## Business Insights

The dashboard enables users to:

* Monitor overall business performance using key KPIs.
* Compare sales performance across regions and product categories.
* Evaluate retailer profitability.
* Analyze customer purchasing channels.
* Identify monthly sales trends and seasonal patterns.
* Filter reports dynamically using City and Gender.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions.
* Increase inventory for top-selling product categories.
* Strengthen partnerships with profitable retailers.
* Improve performance in low-performing sales channels.
* Use monthly sales trends for better inventory and demand planning.

---

## Conclusion

This dashboard demonstrates how Power BI can transform raw sales data into actionable business insights. By combining interactive visualizations with meaningful KPIs, the project supports better business decisions and provides a clear understanding of sales performance across multiple dimensions.
